The PLP ADD TO CART CTA expands an inline quick-buy panel instead of directly adding to cart, which may confuse users expecting a direct add.

Low Cart remove affordance /cart/bag

Initial remove control is an unlabeled icon; removal is possible but the affordance is weak until the confirmation sheet appears.

Low Order tracking validation copy /order-tracking

Blank submit shows a generic Invalid Order Id message instead of clearer required-field guidance.

UI Issues

High Copy / English /order-tracking

Heading reads Where is Order Id? (awkward, inconsistent). Also duplicated below CTA.

Recommendation: Use Where is your order ID? or Track your order, remove duplicate line.

Medium Copy / Grammar /product/pbks-varsity-jacket-17632075

Helper text says Price inclusive of all tax.

Recommendation: Replace with Price inclusive of all taxes.

Medium Copy / Taxonomy /products

Filter rail exposes internal-looking labels (Size Depth, L1 Category, L2 Category).

Recommendation: Rename to business-friendly terms or hide internal taxonomy filters.

Medium Responsive / Readability / (mobile)

Footer links and legal text are cramped/small on mobile.

Recommendation: Increase font-size and spacing; improve grouping/tap targets.

Low Layout / Alignment /brands

Single oversized brand card pinned left; page looks empty/unbalanced.

Recommendation: Adjust grid or container width; center single-brand state.

Low Layout / Visual balance /order-tracking

Small module near top; large empty gap before footer.

Recommendation: Add helpful content or tighten layout to feel intentional.
